# Product Context

## Why This Project Exists
The Kids Menu Ordering App was created to solve the problem of children struggling to communicate their meal choices in restaurants, cafes, or at home. Traditional menus are not designed for kids, and the ordering process can be overwhelming or confusing.

## Problems It Solves
1. **Kid-unfriendly menus**: Traditional menus have small text, complex descriptions, and no visual aids
2. **Order confusion**: Kids struggle to articulate what they want
3. **Parent oversight**: Parents need visibility and control over what kids order
4. **Kitchen efficiency**: Orders need to be clearly communicated to kitchen staff

## How It Works
1. **Profile Selection**: Child selects their avatar/profile from a colorful gateway screen
2. **Guided Ordering**: App walks kids through selecting mains, sides, and drinks in sequence
3. **Optional Items**: Desserts and snacks are available but not part of the required flow
4. **Order Submission**: Once selections are complete, the order is sent to the kitchen
5. **Parent Dashboard**: Parents can view all orders, manage menu items, and track order status

## User Experience Goals
- **Fun and Engaging**: Duolingo-style gamification with progress tracking, confetti celebrations
- **Simple and Intuitive**: Large buttons, clear visual hierarchy, minimal text
- **Safe and Controlled**: Parent PIN protection for admin functions
- **Fast and Responsive**: Smooth animations, instant feedback on selections
- **Mobile-First**: PWA capabilities for use on tablets and phones

## Target Users
- **Primary**: Children ages 3-12 who are ordering meals
- **Secondary**: Parents who need to manage orders and menu items
- **Tertiary**: Kitchen staff who receive and fulfill orders
